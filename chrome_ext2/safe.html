Privacy & Security

G-Recorder Chrome Extension is committed to protecting and preserving the privacy of the user when using unaltered software downloaded from Google Chrome store.

We do not collect any personal information. We also do not collect any browser history of visits to Google maps or any other websites. All the results presented to you are stored one your local computer only and are never send over internet connection.

Third party links

All the links supplied on the results page, such as links to Indeed.com, Hunter.com, etc, should be use at your own personal discretion. They are included to simplify your workflow and do not contain any login credentials. Hence you need to login manually into any of those websites in order utilize the functionality. No information about login process is captured by the G-Recorder.

Any changes we may make to our privacy policy in the future will be posted on the Chrome Extension website, where you obtained this software.
